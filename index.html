Manifesto Pillars
Our Agenda for Ugenya

What We Will Fight For in Parliament

Thirteen locally-rooted pillars drawn from the real needs of Ukwala, North, East and West Ugenya wards.

🛣️
Roads & Connectivity
Lobby and fast-track tarmacking of six key roads that have long denied Ugenya communities access and economic opportunity:
  • 🔹 Ligega–Bondo Kolalo–Bar Ober
  • 🔹 Sega–Jera Junction
  • 🔹 Sega–Ukwala
  • 🔹 Sega–Siranga–Awendo
  • 🔹 Sega–Aboke–Obet
  • 🔹 Ukwala–Aboke–Ligingo
No more impassable murram roads cutting off villages during rainy season. Every road fully funded and fully accounted for.
Infrastructure
💧
Clean Water for Every Home
Advocate for piped water infrastructure to reach all 26 sub-locations. Work with KIWASCO and the county to eliminate reliance on unsafe wells and long water walks — especially for women and girls.
Water & Sanitation
⚡
Electricity to Every Village
Champion last-mile electrification for all unconnected villages including Masiro and surrounding areas. Pursue solar energy subsidies for households too remote for the national grid.
Energy
🌐
Internet Access & Digital Kenya
Push for affordable broadband and public Wi-Fi hotspots in all trading centres. Equip schools with computer labs and ensure youth can access government digital services without travelling to Nairobi.
Digital Access
🎓
Education & Bursaries
Maximise the NG-CDF bursary kitty to ensure every Form 1–4 student and university student from Ugenya gets funded. Build more labs, classrooms and libraries — especially in day schools left behind by national investment.
Education
🏆
Tech Scholarship for Top Students
Establish an annual scholarship programme for the top-performing KCPE and KCSE students in Ugenya in the field of Technology. Scholars will be trained and mentored by global leading tech companies — including firms in Silicon Valley, Nairobi's tech ecosystem and beyond — across software engineering, AI, cybersecurity, data science and cloud computing. Ugenya's brightest minds deserve world-class opportunities.
Innovation
🏛️
Multipurpose Library, Huduma Centre & Digital Tech Hub
Champion construction of a state-of-the-art multi-mega facility in Ugenya housing:
  • 📚 A modern public library with digital and physical resources
  • 🏢 A fully equipped Huduma Centre for efficient public service delivery
  • 💻 A digital tech hub for innovation, entrepreneurship and remote work
One landmark building that brings knowledge, government services and opportunity under one roof — serving every citizen in all four wards.
Public Infrastructure
🎓
Elevate Ugenya TVET to University College
Spearhead the elevation and transformation of the Ugenya TVET Institute into a state-of-the-art university college. This will bring degree-level education, research facilities and industry partnerships directly to Ugenya — ensuring our youth no longer have to leave the constituency to access quality higher education.
Higher Education
💼
Youth Jobs & Enterprise
Create a Ugenya Youth Enterprise Fund through NG-CDF. Partner with KEPSA to bring internships and apprenticeships directly to Ugenya's young people — putting money and opportunity in the hands of the youth.
Youth & Economy
🏥
Healthcare & Dispensaries
Fund full equipment for all sub-county health facilities. Push for 24-hour maternity services in every ward. Advocate for the Linda Mama program to reach every pregnant woman in Ugenya without barriers.
Health
👩🏾
Women & Widows Empowerment
Create a structured women's economic fund to support women-led chamas, widows and single mothers with grants and training. No woman in Ugenya should be homeless or destitute. Push for the Women Enterprise Fund to reach all wards.
Gender Equity
⚽
Sports & Talent Academy
Establish a dedicated Ugenya Sports and Talent Academy to identify, nurture and develop young athletes, musicians, artists and performers. Partner with Football Kenya Federation, Athletics Kenya and the creative industry to give every talented child a clear pathway — from the village to the national stage.
Sports & Talent
🔍
Oversight & Transparency
Actively participate in parliamentary oversight committees to ensure all funds meant for Ugenya — NG-CDF, roads, health — are properly disbursed. Publish a public NG-CDF expenditure portal accessible by every resident. Hold quarterly ward-level accountability forums broadcast on local radio and social media — no more silence from Parliament.
Accountability

About the Candidate

Meet Felix Sawo

⚡ Independent Candidate
"Born and raised in Ugenya — not a visitor, not a politician from the city. One of our own."

Felix Sawo is a homegrown son of Ugenya running as an Independent candidate for Member of Parliament. Untied to party politics and free from political obligations, Felix is answerable to one group only — the people of Ugenya. His vision is rooted in real development: roads, water, digital access, education and jobs that transform every ward.

Roots

Community
